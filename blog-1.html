Clean Install Debloated Windows 10

Windows 10 comes with a lot of bundled bloatware, which hogs down system resources. In this guide we remove bloatware from the windows ISO using the MSMG toolkit and would be integrating the drivers for a ready to go experience out of the box.

Contents:

  1. Explaination
  2. Prerequisites
  3. Procedure

1. Explaination

Since, the launch of Windows 10, users have complained about the level of polish and bloatware it comes with (Candy Crush), not to mention the performance hit it brings due to the increased background activity.

We have made a guide by combining the best debloating tools available for Windows 10, and tested the stability of the same.

We were able to reduce the RAM consumption by almost 1 GB, which if you have 8 GB, is a lot. Also, the background tasks were reduced thus the increase in battery life and the processing power available to other tasks.
